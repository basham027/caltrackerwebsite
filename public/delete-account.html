How to Delete Your Account

If you’d like to remove your CapCal AI account, just follow these steps:

  • On the Home screen, tap the Settings (gear) icon in the top right corner.
  • Scroll to the bottom of the Settings menu and select Delete Account.
  • Tap Delete Account again in the confirmation popup to permanently erase your account.
Note: This action is permanent and will remove all your data from CapCal AI.
